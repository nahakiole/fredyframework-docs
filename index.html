Fredyframework

An amazing framework for your application.

A new player to the game: Fredyframework. It's a fast and amazing framework built to work with the developer and not against him. The framework lets you use whatever components you like, while still giving you a stable ground to build apps at an amazing speed. And of course it's open-source and licenced und the MIT License
